OUR STORY

An independent press rooted in the light and salt of the California coast.

Monterey Shore Ink was founded on a simple conviction: that great books are made slowly, by hand and by heart. We publish a small, deliberate list each year — championing new writers and enduring stories alike, and giving every one the attention of a lighthouse keeper watching the shore.
